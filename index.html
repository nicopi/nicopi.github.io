About me

I am a Marie Skłodowska-Curie ESR and I am working on the EU H2020 SCAVENGE project at the Centre Tecnològic de Telecomunicacions de Catalunya (CTTC) in Barcelona, Spain.
I have a BSc degree in Information Engineering, a MSc in Telecommunication Engineering (both achieved at the University of Padova, Italy) and I am currently a PhD student at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC).
